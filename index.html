About Us

Our Mission

Mahila Prashikshan Samiti is dedicated to empowering women through comprehensive education and skill development programs. We believe that educated women can transform families, communities, and society at large.

Since our inception, we have been committed to providing accessible, quality education and vocational training to women from all backgrounds, helping them achieve economic independence and social empowerment.
